ABOUT US
The CS department's Computer Academy has been around since 1997. Labyrinth was created to give this academy a front end. Labyrinth, the only Computer Science Club of Christ University was created with the sole purpose of giving students a platform to showcase their talents and explore various topics in the Computer Science world.
Labyrinth holds a variety of events every semester, ranging from technical events like coding and debugging competitions, logical puzzle challenges, web development/designing contests to non-technical events such as fastest fingers, gaming and so on.
Labyrinth is for the students, by the Students!

So grab the opportunity to show case your talents!

CLUBS
Labyrinth motivates the students not only knowledgeable in all domains, instead, be an expert in a very specific domain.

The main objective of Labyrinth is to nurture the students potential to develop applications in their preferred technology.

To promote this idea, Labyrinth is having seven clubs as follow:
TIgniculus will organise and execute all the events of the Department. This club plans to create, a platform for the students to exhibit their talents.
Labyrinth - Igniculus (Event Management Club)
Objective of the club is to conduct TED talk shows, Meet Ups, Road Shows and Hackathon.
Labyrinth - StackDomini (IT connect)
This club will help students who are passionate in creating mobile applications and making our life easier.
Labyrinth - Skyacrates (Mobile techies)
Pydruid is a wide platform for students, having major interest in python for creating applications, websites and so on.
Labyrinth - Pydruid (Python club)
CubeX will be focussing on the activities related to robotics, transferring of data through sensors and IoT solutions.
Labyrinth - CubeX (IoT club)
Metaverse will organize more activities for the web developers which will enable them to make excellent web applications, with innovative ideas.
Labyrinth - Metaverse (Web techies club)
Hyponet will make the students to explore their research skills which includes identification of research areas and get to know various research tools.
Labyrinth - Hyponet (Research buddies club)
